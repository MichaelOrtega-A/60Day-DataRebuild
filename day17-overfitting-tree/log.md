### ✅ Day 17 – Understanding Overfitting with Decision Trees

**📅 Date:** June 27, 2025  
**📂 Folder:** `day17-overfitting-tree`

---

**📘 Topics Covered:**
- Reviewed the concept of **overfitting** and why it’s harmful to model performance.
- Compared an **overfitted Decision Tree** with a regular one using accuracy on train/test splits.
- Learned how to **visualize decision trees** and interpret the decision path.
- Practiced explaining how predictions are made using a real example from the tree.

---

**🧪 Results:**
- **Overfitting Tree:**  
  Training Accuracy: **98.03%**  
  Test Accuracy: **78.21%**

- **Regular Tree:**  
  Training Accuracy: **83.43%**  
  Test Accuracy: **79.89%**

✅ Observed how overfitting leads to high training accuracy but worse generalization on unseen data.

---

**💡 Takeaways:**
- Overfitting happens when a model memorizes training data too well.
- Simpler models often perform **better on test data** by avoiding noise.
- Decision trees can be controlled using parameters like `max_depth`.
