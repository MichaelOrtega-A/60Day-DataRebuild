# Week 1 Reflection – Why I Won’t Stop This Time

I’ve failed at consistency before, but I’m not that version of myself anymore.  
This week, I showed up, built real projects, and started proving to myself that I belong in this space.  
I’m tired of feeling like I’m wasting my potential. I know this won’t be easy — but I’d rather struggle through this than stay stuck making $17/hour with no direction.

I don’t hate my job — in fact, I’m grateful for it.  
It taught me resilience, gave me structure when I needed it most, and reminded me how blessed I am to even pursue a career I love.  
That job paid my bills after I got laid off, and being around people who don’t have the same opportunities made me realize I should strive for more — because I *can*.  
I had taken that privilege for granted.

But I know I’m meant for more — and this time, I’m not letting go of the version of me that keeps showing up.  
I’m all in.
