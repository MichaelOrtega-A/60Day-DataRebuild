# Day 14 – Model Interpretation

Today I focused on learning how to interpret what machine learning models are actually doing.

I didn’t rely on charts — instead, I printed out the raw **feature importances** from the Random Forest and the **coefficients** from Logistic Regression.

It finally started to click:
- **Random Forest** tells me what features matter most in decision-making.
- **Logistic Regression** shows me how features push predictions up or down (like increasing or decreasing the odds of survival).

Seeing those plain numbers helped me realize: I don’t need to memorize code. I need to **understand what it's telling me**, and today was a big step forward.

Tomorrow I’ll explore visualizations once I’ve digested this part more.
