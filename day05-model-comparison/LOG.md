## Logistic Regression Insights
- Sex had the strongest positive influence (+2.547), meaning females had much higher survival odds
- Pclass and IsAlone had strong negative influences, decreasing survival chances

## Decision Tree Insights
- The tree splits first on Sex, then Age (for females) or Pclass (for males)
- The structure matches historical reality of evacuation priorities (women, children, first class)
